How to play.

  1. Press the up arrow key to fly up
  2. Press down arrow key to fly down
  3. Press UP and DOWN arrows repeatedly to keep steady
  4. Press SPACEBAR to fly stright or steady (this will cost you 1 point)
  5. You can also Use "W" to fly up or "S" to fly down

Rules.

  1. Don't touch the top or bottom of the screen
  2. Dont fly into any obstacles in the air
  3. You receive 1 point for every obstacle you go past
  4. You forfeit 1 point each time you press SPACEBAR to fly steady
